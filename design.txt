Title: Flatmates Bill
Description: An app that gets as inputs the anoumt of bill for a particular period
and return how mach each of the flatmate has to pay. It also generates a PDF report 
stating the names of the flatmates,the period, and how mach each of them had to pay.
Objects: Bill:
           anoumt
           period
        flatmate:
           name
           days_in_house
           pay(bill)
        PdfReport:
        filename
        generate(flatmate1,flatmate2, bill)
